## Relevant Industry Standards or Protocols

- **APEX/ANSI Event Industry Standards** — industry-wide standards for event management processes, terminology, and documentation published by Events Industry Council
- **iCalendar (RFC 5545)** — calendar data format for syncing venue availability across systems and with clients
- **PCI DSS** — payment security standard governing deposit, invoice, and booking payment processing
- **AV over IP (SDVoE, AVB/TSN)** — network-based AV distribution standards increasingly relevant to venue AV management modules
- **GDPR / CCPA** — privacy regulations governing attendee and client data collected through booking and event management systems

## Market Research

**Market Size:** The event venue management software segment operates within the broader event management software market, estimated at USD 15.2 billion globally in 2026, with venue-specific tooling representing a meaningful sub-segment driven by post-pandemic recovery in live events and corporate gatherings.

**Funding:** Momentus Technologies (Ungerboeck + Priava) raised significant private equity backing post-merger. Tripleseat raised $84M from Bessemer Venture Partners. HoneyBook raised over $250M across multiple rounds.

**Pricing Landscape:** Entry-level tools (HoneyBook, Planning Pod) start at $19–99/month. Mid-market venue platforms (Tripleseat, Skedda) use custom pricing typically in the $300–1,500/month range. Enterprise platforms (Momentus, Ungerboeck) are six-figure annual contracts.

**Key Buyer Personas:** Independent event venues (wedding barns, conference suites), restaurant and hotel event departments, convention centre operations teams, corporate campus facility managers, and university event offices.

**Notable Trends:** Hybrid event coordination (simultaneous in-person and virtual) requiring AV and streaming integration; self-service booking portals to reduce sales friction; integrated digital BEO (Banquet Event Order) workflows replacing paper processes; revenue management for off-peak space utilisation; sustainability tracking for event carbon footprint reporting.

## AI-Native Opportunity

- AI-powered lead qualification that scores inbound enquiries, proposes available dates, and generates personalised proposals without sales staff involvement
- Automated BEO generation from natural language event briefs, pulling room configurations, catering menus, AV requirements, and staffing into structured documents
- Dynamic space pricing that adjusts room rates based on demand, seasonal patterns, competing events in the local market, and time-to-event urgency
- Intelligent catering optimisation — predicting attendee dietary requirements, minimising food waste through portion forecasting, and generating grocery orders
- Post-event AI analysis aggregating client feedback, staff notes, and financial data to surface operational improvements and identify upsell opportunities for future bookings
