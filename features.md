## Cross-Cutting Feature Themes

### Table-Stakes Features

- Event booking and venue availability management
- Space/room layout and floor plan management
- Customer CRM and lead tracking
- Contract and agreement management
- Invoicing and payment processing
- Event timeline and task management
- Catering and menu integration
- Post-event reporting and analytics

### Differentiating Features

- AI lead qualification and proposal generation
- Automated BEO (Banquet Event Order) generation
- Dynamic space pricing and revenue management
- Catering workflow and production scheduling
- Self-serve booking portal
- AV and technical equipment management
- Hybrid event (in-person + virtual) coordination
- Sustainability and carbon tracking

### Underserved Areas

- End-to-end operational intelligence and alerts
- Real-time resource allocation and optimisation
- Predictive operational issues (staffing, catering, AV)
- Automated compliance and health/safety workflows
- Post-event AI analysis and insights
- Supplier and vendor relationship management
- Dynamic pricing with demand forecasting

### AI-Augmentation Candidates

- Lead qualification and personalised proposals
- Automated BEO generation from natural language briefs
- Dynamic space pricing based on demand and seasonality
- Predictive catering requirements and food waste reduction
- Post-event analysis and upsell opportunities
- Staffing and resource forecasting
